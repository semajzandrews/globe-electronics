# Globe Electronics — REFERENCE (external award-grade, fresh per Blocklist Law)

Researched June 2026 for the cell-phone/electronics-repair vertical. The bar is external;
no internal build was used as reference.

## What current award-grade retail/editorial does (Awwwards / SiteInspire 2025-26)
1. **Oversized display typography as the layout** — type IS the composition, poster-style,
   serifs/display faces dominating (Awwwards 2025 typography trend). I lean into this with
   Erode at monumental scale for chapter words.
2. **Editorial storytelling over carousels** (Aesop-school) — immersive photography + elegant
   type + a narrative spine, not a product grid. Maps to my pinned repair-story.
3. **Sticky / pinned scroll choreography** — a fixed stage with advancing chapters is a current,
   premium scroll mechanic when it carries real content (not gimmick scroll-jack). I keep normal
   smooth scroll everywhere else; only the signature stage pins.
4. **Material warmth + restraint** — one confident accent, graded photography to a single wash,
   generous negative space. Avoids the tech-store neon/blue cliche.

## Category cliches BANNED for this build (Novelty)
- Blue-to-cyan tech gradients, circuit-trace-as-decoration wallpaper, carrier-store red/yellow,
  glossy "we fix it fast!!" stock with checkmark badges, neon-on-black gamer aesthetic,
  stacked phone-render hero on white. None appear here.

## Direction chosen
Trust vertical (people hand a stranger a device they care about) -> **realness-forward**:
real repair-bench photography is the medium, warm graded, Erode editorial type, burnt-orange
as the single revive-glow accent, sticky teardown-to-revive as the one signature moment.
